About Me

I am a computer science student currently studying at Queen's University Belfast, hoping to graduate in the coming months with an MEng. I have been accepted into a PhD role at EPCC with the University of Edinburgh, where I will be working on heterogenous computing in HPC.

Within the field, I have been lucky to attend the ISC HPC Conference in Hamburg in 2024 as a student volunteer, and have been working as a research assistant at the Centre for Secure Information Technologies (CSIT) at Queen's University Belfast. I also have an interest in novel technologoies.

Outside of my studies, I have a huge interest in sport (every sport you can think of), and I have also been getting into my fitness, with a goal to hit a 25 minute 5KM in the next few months and to run a half marathon by the end of the year. I also have a passion for literature and have recently began writing my own poems and wanted to share them.#
